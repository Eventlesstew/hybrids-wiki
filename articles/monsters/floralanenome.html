Floral Anenome?

Biology

Floral Anemone, also known as Florie (I know it's not really an Anemone, but it does use Green Anemone DNA, shut up) is more of a muddled combination of Green Anemones and Florella's species than the wonders I've created. While seemingly more reserved than Florella, Floral Anemone is still far from being a prim, proper, put-together princess, she enjoys going around the Sanctuary's forest and seeing it's wonders and using some colour berries I gave her to make some art. Silly gal even got some paint on herself, which she decided to leave on and even painted her tail to look like a bee.
