Boomin' Anenome

Biology

You thought plants only lived on the ground? Well, this one ain't and clouds are basically water so she's good hydration wise. (Good thing I made the Sanctuary big enough to fit some clouds in) Boomin' Anemone, also known as Boomy, is a floating flower who enjoys making art just as much as her sister, Floral Anemone (The coloured petals were a result of her messing around with Colour berries) and she also likes playing on her drum kit. There are also times where parts of Boomin' Anemone's petals condense into water droplets which is caught by the raincloud below her and makes the same sound Blowy's dew drops make. Fascinating!
