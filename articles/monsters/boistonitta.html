Biology

A silly gal who enjoys the simple things in life, Boistonitta enjoys plucking the strings on her horns which makes different sounds depending on if she's using something else (Mainly the cloud pick) to pluck the strings or not. The crystals around Boistonitta's shell also help produce some percussion sounds which have some added reverb thanks to the slime. Boistonitta likes drawing things using the colour berries I originally gave to the Anenome Sisters, which is how Florie became besties with Boistonitta. Judging by how many of her childish drawings (no offense, hope she doesn't find this) are around the part of the cave with a little skylight, it seems like that's where she likes to go for shelter, so I wouldn't be surprised if she also befriended Glactigloop or Phansmazt. (Floureese doesn't really go out of the caves much, he's more of a dweller than the others.)
