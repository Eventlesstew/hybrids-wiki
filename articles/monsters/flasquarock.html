Flasquarock is quite weird. The glass floats and moves together and it seems like the lava has properties similar to Flasque's lifeblood in them, keeping them from shattering. In terms of behavior, which is not as weird thankfully, Flasquarock prefers being alone, but he is okay with having some company, preferably people who are usually quiet and chill.
