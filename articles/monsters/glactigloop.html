Glactigloop

Biology

Glactis and Cosmogloops are… really freaking weird, and I'm talking about their biology. Glactigloop has both of their weirdnesses and the result is an interesting creature who has the best of both! They're really freaking powerful as echolocation and having one eye open is a deadly mix, only strengthened further thanks to Glactigloop seemingly being able to see the future. They're also capable of shapeshifting their body when needed, which really helps when fitting through small passages, good thing I replaced the vent covers with ones that they can't slide their way through though. They're also not as evil as they seem at first glance, they're loyal to others and are rather protective of their friends in Hybrid Sanctuary too.
