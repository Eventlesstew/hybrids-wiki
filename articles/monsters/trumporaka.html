Biology

Trumporaka is the unique result of a Single Element monster mixed with a Quad Element monster, Trumporaka enjoys creating stuff out of clay and even adding gems on top of them, which I've started growing in the Sanctuary's caves to make sure he feels welcome. He even built his own version of Clay Kiln with a few gems added on top of it, which looks eerily similar to the Clay Kiln that stood before it was burned down... Was this an uncanny coincidence, or does that DNA have something else in it?
